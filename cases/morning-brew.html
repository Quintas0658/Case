What Morning Brew Does

The Problem

Traditional business news was "dry and dense" - WSJ, Bloomberg, and other outlets created content that felt intimidating and inaccessible to young professionals and students trying to stay informed about business and finance.

Pain Point: "Business news felt like homework, not something I wanted to read"

The Solution

A daily newsletter that makes business news "engaging and accessible" with a conversational, witty tone. Complex financial topics explained like a smart friend breaking down the news over coffee.

Value Prop: "Your witty friend explaining business news"
