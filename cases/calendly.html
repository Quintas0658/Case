Product

What Calendly Does

The Problem

Scheduling meetings involved endless email back-and-forth: "How about Tuesday?" "I'm busy, what about Wednesday?" Sales teams, recruiters, and consultants wasted hours coordinating calendars instead of doing their actual work.

Pain Point: "It took 8 emails to schedule a 30-minute call"

The Solution

One-click scheduling with automatic calendar integration. Share your availability link, recipients pick a time that works, and the meeting appears in both calendars. No accounts required for booking.

Value Prop: "Turn scheduling from 8 emails into 1 click"

Wrong Channels

Effortful push, weak conversion

Cold outreach
  • Cold emails and LinkedIn DMs were largely ignored
  • High cost, low conversion rates
  • Hard to explain the value without showing it
Paid advertising
  • Expensive customer acquisition costs
  • Hard to target users at the right moment
  • Scheduling is situational, not always top-of-mind
Why it failed

These channels didn't let recipients experience the scheduling magic instantly.

Result: Slow growth, high CAC, difficulty scaling

Right Channel

The link is the channel

Viral loop

  • Zero friction: recipients don't need to register to schedule
  • "Powered by Calendly" embedded across the flow
  • Perfect timing: people see Calendly when they need scheduling
Use cases expand
  • Sales: Demo scheduling, follow-up calls
  • Recruiting: Interview coordination
  • Customer Success: Onboarding, check-ins
  • Education: Office hours, student meetings
  • Each booking introduces new teams to Calendly

Result: Self-sustaining growth, $0 CAC for viral users
